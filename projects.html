Synergistic Communications with High Resiliency

Links:

  • Full Research Paper
  • Literature Review

Goals:

  • Mitigate interference in wireless 5G communication between Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and users
  • Create a MATLAB simulation that calculates interference in realistic scenarios

Methods Used:

  • Collaborative Beamforming
    • Direct the signal in the direction of the desired user
    • Decreases interference in terrestrial areas by providing a more direct route to a user
  • Maximum Ratio Combining (MRC)
    • The weighted signals of each antenna are summed together into a combined signal
    • This method helps maximize the received signal quality

Conclusions

  • This is the first simulation that calculates interference in a realistic scenario
  • The dynamic simulation accurately displays the relationship between distance and interference
  • UAVs are becoming more effective with distributing reliable communication with high resiliency
